f8 fc

Nov 28, 2024

The open-source brushed AIO flight controller for x8 drones.

f8 fc is a fully open source flight controller that is made primarily for small drones with brushed drones. It features:

  • STM32F405 microcontroller with 1MB flash
  • BMI270 imu
  • BMP388 barometer
  • Onboard 16MB external flash for data logging
  • Status LEDs
  • 1-4s input voltage
  • 4 available UARTs
  • 8 brushed motor drives with 6A mosfets
  • Voltage & current sensing
